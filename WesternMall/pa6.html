Marvin™

Meet Marvin™ your new personal assistant. He will iron your clothes, answer your comm, take messages, and overall make your life easier. With his IGotzEmotions™ chip, he will act and react to your wishes and whims with real personality. Additional features and functions include:

  • ChefRamsey™ protocols will ensure that you always have delicious and unique dishes to eat during mealtimes.

  • IllChargeIt™ ports will allow Marvin™ to charge many of your devices.

  • IllBurnItDown™ technology allows Marvin to use his built in lighter as a defensive flame thrower, protecting you and your home from intruders.

Marvin™ will make the ideal personal assistant for anyone that doesn't want to spend big Terran Units on more expensive units.
