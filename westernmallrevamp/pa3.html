Synergy™

  • LookI'mFlying™ technologies use magnetics and tiny thrusters to keep Synergy moving and in the air and the ICanzDodgeAnything™ protocols allow Synergy to never get snared by hanging wires, curtains, or close range gunshots and shrapnel.

  • Synergy's YouAreEverywhereNow™ transmitter sends captured holo media to the IAMGOD™ satellite network, which then broadcasts it to all the networks you have subscribed to.

  • Synergy is not just your personal assistant, but your loving manager. Ensuring you make it to all scheduled gigs by using it's built-in WheresMyRide™* protocols which finds the closest armored limo service and contracts them to get you to your next gig.

With Synergy on your side, you are sure to be a shooting star.

WesternMall FAQs


What is the WesternMall?

(OOC) It is a website where players can spend their hard earned Terran Units. All equipment available in the game will be able to be shopped for here at the WesternMall website.

(IC) It's only the best place to shop for the serious merc. Things that go BOOM, things that keep you from going BOOM, and a few things to make your life easier are all available here at the WesternMall.


*OOC is out of character and IC is In Character

Why a website?

Three reasons:

  • It's easier than sharing a book.
  • It allows for more items to be added over time without publishing a new book.
  • It adds a lot more immersion to the game.
