TinHead™

TinHead is the top of the line personal assistant from VirtualTech Corp. With TinHead's state-of-the-art neuro brain with IGotThisCovered™ protocols, staying organized has never been such a breeze. With features such as:

  • ICanzRememberAnything™ memory modules ensures there is room for your schedule and still room for the comm number of every individual on the planet.

  • IiZNinja™ protocols allows TinHead to turn anything into a weapon, providing security for your home or living space.

  • TinHead's poly-carbon armor protects him from the stray bullet and small grenade blasts.

  • The SliceNDice™ protocol provides amazing meals provided you have the groceries to do so and also is usable on your enemies.

  • The NowI'mMad™ self destruction system completely ensures that your data and your enemy's life are going nowhere.

  • TinHead's I'sShopping™ system allows your TinHead to buy and sell merchandise for you while you are away.

WesternMall FAQs


  • What is the WesternMall?

    (OOC) It is a website where players can spend their hard earned Terran Units. All equipment available in the game will be able to be shopped for here at the WesternMall website.

    (IC) It's only the best place to shop for the serious merc. Things that go BOOM, things that keep you from going BOOM, and a few things to make your life easier are all available here at the WesternMall.

    *OOC is out of character and IC is In Character

  • Why a website?

    Three reasons:

    It's easier than sharing a book.
    It allows for more items to be added over time without publishing a new book.
    It adds a lot more immersion to the game.

  • Do you have a life outside of game designing and developing?

    No, I do not.

  • What are the future plans for this site?

    A lot more items, character accounts with GM access to award TU for characters to spend, and shipping manifests to be sent to player email to prove they purchased the items.
