Hal™

Hal, your next gen personal assistant is the result of a collaboration between a little known corp known as FunTech and WarTech. FunTech's neuroprocessing unit with personality sub-processing, makes your new personal assistant quirky and fun to be around. It's value packed with the following technologies:

  • IGotNeedzToo™ protocols reminds you that this personal assistant is more than your assistant, but your friend.

  • ItsYurTurnToMakeDinner™ protocols ensures you do not become too reliant on this amazing new assistant you have purchased.

  • IKnowKungFoo™ architecture enables your personal assistant to protect your home or living space from any and all assailants. This is bolstered by IllPullYourHair™, HamString™, and JuggularIncision™ protocols.

  • ImWatchingU™ technology ensures that all your calls are fielded and the least important are weeded out for you. Only receive the calls that matter.

  • FastFoodAgain?™ protocols will ensure that you don't order too much fast food and gain weight that can slow you down and drain your CredAccount. ToFuIncoming!™ protocol will intercept any fast food order and replace it with a healthy alternative.
